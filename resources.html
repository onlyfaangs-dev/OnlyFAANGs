Job Search Checklist Before you apply
Week 1 — Prep
  • 01Update your resume with measurable impact statements
  • 02Clean up your LinkedIn — headline, summary, featured section
  • 03Define your target role, level, and 10–15 companies
  • 04Research compensation ranges on Levels.fyi and Glassdoor
  • 05Set up job alerts on OnlyFAANGs for your target companies
Week 2–3 — Apply
  • 06Tailor each resume to the job description — match keywords
  • 07Apply directly on company career pages — not third-party boards
  • 08Reach out to a current employee at each target company
  • 09Track every application in a spreadsheet with dates and status
  • 10Follow up after 1 week if no response from recruiter
Week 4–5 — Interview & Close
  • 11Prep 5 strong STAR stories covering leadership, conflict, and impact
  • 12Do at least 3 mock interviews before your first real one
  • 13Send a thank-you email within 24h of every interview
  • 14Never accept an offer on the spot — always ask for time
  • 15Negotiate — base, signing bonus, equity, and start date
Interview Playbook For FAANG & Fortune 500
01
Research the Company Deeply
Go beyond the homepage. Read earnings calls, engineering blogs, and recent press. Know their current strategic priorities and how your role fits in.
02
Master the STAR Method
Every behavioral question at a Fortune 500 expects structured answers. Situation, Task, Action, Result — practice until it's effortless and specific.
03
Prepare for System Design
Senior engineering roles at FAANG weigh system design heavily. Practice designing scalable systems out loud. Think in trade-offs, not correct answers.
04
Always Negotiate
The first offer is rarely the best offer. Big tech companies expect negotiation — especially on equity. A competing offer is the single most effective lever.
